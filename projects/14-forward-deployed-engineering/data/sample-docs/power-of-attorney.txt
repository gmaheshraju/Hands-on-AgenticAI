DURABLE POWER OF ATTORNEY

I, the undersigned Principal, hereby appoint my Agent (Attorney-in-Fact) to act on my
behalf with full authority as described herein.

1. GRANT OF AUTHORITY
I grant my Agent the following powers:
(a) Financial Management: To manage my bank accounts, investments, real property, and
personal property, including the power to buy, sell, lease, mortgage, and otherwise
deal with any property, real or personal;
(b) Tax Matters: To prepare, sign, and file tax returns, make estimated tax payments,
and represent me before the Internal Revenue Service and state tax authorities;
(c) Legal Proceedings: To commence, prosecute, discontinue, or defend any legal
proceedings on my behalf, including the power to retain counsel and settle claims;
(d) Government Benefits: To apply for and manage government benefits including Social
Security, Medicare, Medicaid, and veterans' benefits;
(e) Insurance: To procure, maintain, modify, or terminate insurance policies of any kind;
(f) Estate Planning: To create, amend, or revoke trusts and make gifts on my behalf,
subject to the limitations set forth in Section 4.

2. DURABILITY
This Power of Attorney shall not be affected by my subsequent disability or incapacity.
It shall remain in full force and effect until revoked by me in writing or upon my death.

3. SUCCESSOR AGENT
If my primary Agent is unable or unwilling to serve, I appoint my Successor Agent to
serve with the same powers and duties.

4. LIMITATIONS
(a) Gift Limitation: My Agent shall not make gifts exceeding the annual federal gift
tax exclusion amount ($18,000 per recipient as of 2024) without court approval;
(b) Self-Dealing: My Agent shall not use my assets for their own benefit unless
specifically authorized;
(c) Real Property: Any sale of real property shall require an independent appraisal
and shall not be below 90% of fair market value;
(d) Healthcare: This Power of Attorney does not grant authority over healthcare decisions
(see separate Healthcare Directive).

5. FIDUCIARY DUTY
My Agent shall act as a fiduciary, exercising the powers granted herein in my best
interest, with due care, loyalty, and in good faith. My Agent shall keep accurate
records of all transactions conducted on my behalf.

6. THIRD-PARTY RELIANCE
Third parties may rely on the authority granted in this Power of Attorney. Any third
party who acts in good faith reliance on this document shall be held harmless from
liability for such reliance.

7. COMPENSATION
My Agent shall be entitled to reasonable compensation for services rendered under this
Power of Attorney, consistent with the prevailing rates for similar services in the
applicable jurisdiction.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Power of Attorney shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.

9. EXECUTION
This Power of Attorney shall be effective immediately upon execution and shall be
witnessed by two disinterested witnesses and acknowledged before a notary public.
