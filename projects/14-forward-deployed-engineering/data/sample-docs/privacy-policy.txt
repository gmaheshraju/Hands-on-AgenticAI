PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Last Updated: March 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how LegalTech Solutions Inc. ("Company," "we," "us," or "our")
collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information obtained through our document
analysis platform ("Service").

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
(a) Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, job title, and employer
information provided during account registration.
(b) Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the Service, including pages
visited, features used, queries submitted, documents uploaded, and session duration.
(c) Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, device identifiers,
and cookie data.
(d) Document Data: Documents uploaded to the Service for analysis, including metadata
such as file name, size, creation date, and author information.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
We use collected information to:
(a) Provide, maintain, and improve the Service;
(b) Process and analyze documents submitted by users;
(c) Communicate with users about their accounts and service updates;
(d) Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats;
(e) Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

3. DATA RETENTION
(a) Account Data: Retained for the duration of the user's account plus 30 days after
account deletion.
(b) Document Data: Documents are processed in real-time and stored for a maximum of
90 days unless the user requests earlier deletion.
(c) Usage Logs: Retained for 12 months for analytics and security purposes.
(d) Legal Hold: Data may be retained beyond standard periods if required by law or
subject to a litigation hold.

4. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE
We do not sell personal information. We may share information with:
(a) Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist in operating the Service, subject
to confidentiality obligations;
(b) Legal Compliance: When required by law, subpoena, or court order;
(c) Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets;
(d) Consent: When the user has provided explicit consent to share information.

5. DATA SECURITY
We implement industry-standard security measures, including:
(a) AES-256 encryption for data at rest;
(b) TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit;
(c) Multi-factor authentication for user accounts;
(d) Regular security audits and penetration testing;
(e) SOC 2 Type II compliance.

6. USER RIGHTS (CCPA / GDPR)
Users have the right to:
(a) Access: Request a copy of their personal information;
(b) Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal information;
(c) Deletion: Request deletion of their personal information;
(d) Portability: Receive their data in a structured, machine-readable format;
(e) Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data);
(f) Non-Discrimination: Exercise privacy rights without discriminatory treatment.

7. COOKIES AND TRACKING
We use essential cookies for Service functionality and optional analytics cookies with
user consent. Users may manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
The Service is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly
collect personal information from children.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material
changes by email or through the Service.

10. CONTACT INFORMATION
For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact our Data Protection Officer at
privacy@legaltechsolutions.com.
