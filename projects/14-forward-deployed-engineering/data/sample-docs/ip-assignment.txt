INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT AGREEMENT

This Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and
between the Assignor and the Assignee identified below.

1. DEFINITIONS
(a) "Assigned IP" means all inventions, discoveries, improvements, designs, works of
authorship, trade secrets, know-how, software, source code, algorithms, databases,
trademarks, and other intellectual property created by the Assignor during the Assignment
Period, whether or not patentable or copyrightable.
(b) "Assignment Period" means the period from January 1, 2024 through the termination
of Assignor's engagement with Assignee.

2. ASSIGNMENT OF RIGHTS
Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to Assignee all right, title,
and interest in and to the Assigned IP, including:
(a) All patent rights, including the right to file and prosecute patent applications worldwide;
(b) All copyrights, including the right to reproduce, distribute, and create derivative works;
(c) All trade secret rights;
(d) All trademark rights associated with the Assigned IP;
(e) All rights to sue for past, present, and future infringement.

3. MORAL RIGHTS WAIVER
To the extent permitted by applicable law, Assignor hereby waives all moral rights
(droit moral) in the Assigned IP, including the right of attribution and the right
of integrity.

4. FURTHER ASSURANCES
Assignor agrees to execute all documents and take all actions reasonably requested by
Assignee to perfect the assignment of the Assigned IP, including executing patent
applications, copyright registrations, and assignment recordations.

5. PRIOR INVENTIONS
Assignor has listed on Exhibit A all inventions, discoveries, and works of authorship
conceived or created by Assignor prior to the Assignment Period ("Prior Inventions").
Prior Inventions are excluded from the Assigned IP.

6. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES
Assignor represents and warrants that:
(a) The Assigned IP is original to the Assignor and does not infringe any third-party
intellectual property rights;
(b) Assignor has not previously assigned, licensed, or encumbered the Assigned IP;
(c) Assignor has the legal right and authority to make this assignment.

7. CONSIDERATION
In consideration for this assignment, Assignee shall pay Assignor the sum of $25,000,
which Assignor acknowledges as adequate consideration.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
