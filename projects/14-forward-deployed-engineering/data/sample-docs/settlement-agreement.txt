SETTLEMENT AGREEMENT AND MUTUAL RELEASE

This Settlement Agreement and Mutual Release ("Agreement") is entered into by and between
DataFlow Corp. ("Plaintiff") and CloudSync Technologies Inc. ("Defendant"), collectively
referred to as the "Parties."

RECITALS
WHEREAS, Plaintiff filed a complaint against Defendant in the United States District Court
for the Northern District of California, Case No. 3:24-cv-01234 ("Litigation"), alleging
patent infringement, trade secret misappropriation, and unfair business practices;

WHEREAS, Defendant denied all allegations and asserted counterclaims for declaratory
judgment of non-infringement and patent invalidity;

WHEREAS, the Parties wish to resolve all disputes between them without further litigation
and without admission of liability by either Party;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises and covenants set forth herein,
the Parties agree as follows:

1. SETTLEMENT PAYMENT
Defendant shall pay Plaintiff the total sum of $3,750,000 ("Settlement Amount") as follows:
(a) $2,000,000 within thirty (30) days of execution of this Agreement;
(b) $1,000,000 within ninety (90) days of execution;
(c) $750,000 within one hundred eighty (180) days of execution.
Late payments shall accrue interest at a rate of 8% per annum.

2. LICENSE GRANT
(a) Plaintiff hereby grants Defendant a non-exclusive, worldwide, perpetual license
to use U.S. Patent Nos. 10,123,456 and 10,789,012 in connection with Defendant's
cloud synchronization products;
(b) The license is non-transferable and non-sublicensable;
(c) The license does not extend to any future patents owned by Plaintiff.

3. MUTUAL RELEASE
(a) Plaintiff Release: Plaintiff hereby releases and discharges Defendant from any and
all claims, demands, causes of action, and liabilities arising from or related to the
Litigation, whether known or unknown;
(b) Defendant Release: Defendant hereby releases and discharges Plaintiff from any and
all counterclaims, demands, causes of action, and liabilities arising from or related
to the Litigation, whether known or unknown;
(c) California Civil Code Section 1542 Waiver: Each Party expressly waives the
provisions of Section 1542, which provides that "a general release does not extend to
claims that the creditor or releasing party does not know or suspect to exist."

4. DISMISSAL OF LITIGATION
Within five (5) business days of receipt of the initial payment, the Parties shall file
a stipulation of dismissal with prejudice of all claims and counterclaims in the Litigation.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
The terms of this Agreement, including the Settlement Amount, shall be kept strictly
confidential by both Parties. Neither Party shall disclose the terms to any third party
except as required by law, regulation, or court order, or to their respective legal
counsel, accountants, and insurers.

6. NON-DISPARAGEMENT
Neither Party shall make any public statement disparaging the other Party, its products,
services, or personnel.

7. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES
Each Party represents and warrants that:
(a) It has the authority to enter into this Agreement;
(b) This Agreement has been duly authorized and executed;
(c) No other person or entity has any interest in the claims released herein.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
