COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Commercial Lease Agreement ("Lease") is entered into between Blackstone Property
Holdings LLC ("Landlord") and TechStart Inc. ("Tenant").

1. PREMISES
Landlord hereby leases to Tenant the premises located at 450 Market Street, Suite 800,
San Francisco, CA 94105, comprising approximately 5,200 square feet of office space
("Premises").

2. LEASE TERM
(a) Initial Term: The Lease shall commence on January 1, 2025 and expire on
December 31, 2029 ("Initial Term"), for a total of five (5) years.
(b) Renewal Option: Tenant shall have the option to renew the Lease for one (1)
additional term of three (3) years by providing written notice at least 180 days prior
to the expiration of the Initial Term.

3. RENT
(a) Base Rent: Tenant shall pay monthly base rent of $28,600, payable on the first
business day of each month.
(b) Annual Escalation: Base rent shall increase by 3% on each anniversary of the
Commencement Date.
(c) Security Deposit: Tenant shall provide a security deposit equal to two (2) months'
base rent ($57,200) upon execution of this Lease.
(d) Triple Net (NNN): In addition to base rent, Tenant shall pay its proportionate share
of property taxes, insurance, and common area maintenance (CAM) charges.

4. PERMITTED USE
The Premises shall be used exclusively for general office purposes, including but not
limited to software development, administrative functions, and client meetings. Tenant
shall not use the Premises for any unlawful purpose or in violation of any applicable
zoning ordinances.

5. TENANT IMPROVEMENTS
Landlord shall provide a tenant improvement allowance of $45 per square foot ($234,000
total) for initial build-out of the Premises. Any improvements exceeding this allowance
shall be at Tenant's expense. All improvements shall become the property of Landlord
upon termination of the Lease.

6. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
Tenant shall maintain the Premises in good condition and repair. Landlord shall be
responsible for structural repairs, roof maintenance, and exterior upkeep. HVAC
maintenance shall be the responsibility of the Tenant.

7. INDEMNIFICATION
Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from any claims, damages, or
liabilities arising from Tenant's use of the Premises, except to the extent caused by
Landlord's negligence or willful misconduct.

8. DEFAULT AND REMEDIES
(a) Tenant Default: Failure to pay rent within ten (10) days of the due date or breach
of any material term shall constitute a default.
(b) Landlord Remedies: Upon default, Landlord may terminate the Lease and recover
possession, accelerate remaining rent, or pursue any other remedies available at law.

9. GOVERNING LAW
This Lease shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
