AGREEMENT AND PLAN OF MERGER

This Agreement and Plan of Merger ("Merger Agreement") is entered into by and between
Apex Technologies Inc. ("Acquiror") and NovaSoft Solutions LLC ("Target Company").

1. THE MERGER
(a) Structure: Target Company shall merge with and into a wholly-owned subsidiary
of Acquiror ("Merger Sub"), with Merger Sub surviving the merger as a wholly-owned
subsidiary of Acquiror (the "Surviving Entity").
(b) Effective Time: The Merger shall become effective upon the filing of the Certificate
of Merger with the Secretary of State of Delaware ("Effective Time").

2. MERGER CONSIDERATION
(a) Purchase Price: The aggregate merger consideration shall be $42,000,000 ("Purchase
Price"), consisting of:
  (i) $30,000,000 in cash payable at Closing;
  (ii) $8,000,000 in Acquiror common stock, valued at the 20-day VWAP prior to Closing;
  (iii) $4,000,000 in an escrow account for indemnification obligations.
(b) Working Capital Adjustment: The Purchase Price shall be adjusted based on the
difference between actual working capital at Closing and a target working capital of
$3,500,000.
(c) Earnout: Additional consideration of up to $6,000,000 payable over 24 months based
on achievement of specified revenue milestones.

3. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES OF TARGET COMPANY
Target Company represents and warrants that:
(a) Organization: Target Company is duly organized and in good standing under Delaware law;
(b) Authority: Target Company has the requisite power to execute this Agreement;
(c) Financial Statements: The financial statements delivered to Acquiror fairly present
the financial condition of Target Company in accordance with GAAP;
(d) Material Contracts: All material contracts have been disclosed to Acquiror;
(e) Intellectual Property: Target Company owns or has valid licenses to all intellectual
property used in its business;
(f) No Litigation: There is no pending or threatened litigation that could have a Material
Adverse Effect.

4. COVENANTS
(a) Conduct of Business: Between signing and Closing, Target Company shall conduct its
business in the ordinary course consistent with past practice;
(b) No-Shop: Target Company shall not solicit, encourage, or enter into discussions with
any third party regarding an alternative acquisition proposal;
(c) Access: Target Company shall provide Acquiror reasonable access to its books, records,
and personnel for due diligence purposes.

5. CONDITIONS TO CLOSING
(a) Regulatory Approvals: Receipt of all required regulatory approvals, including
Hart-Scott-Rodino antitrust clearance;
(b) Material Adverse Effect: No Material Adverse Effect shall have occurred between
signing and Closing;
(c) Third-Party Consents: Receipt of all required third-party consents.

6. INDEMNIFICATION
(a) Target Company shall indemnify Acquiror for breaches of representations, warranties,
and covenants, subject to a deductible of $500,000 and a cap of $8,000,000;
(b) The escrow fund shall serve as the primary source of indemnification payments.

7. TERMINATION
This Agreement may be terminated:
(a) By mutual written consent;
(b) By either party if the Closing has not occurred within 120 days of the date hereof;
(c) By Acquiror if a Material Adverse Effect occurs.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
