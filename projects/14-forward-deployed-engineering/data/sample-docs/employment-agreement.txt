EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into by and between
Acme Legal Corp. ("Employer") and the Employee identified on the signature page.

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
Employee shall serve as Senior Associate in the Corporate Law Division. Employee's
duties shall include drafting corporate governance documents, reviewing merger and
acquisition agreements, conducting due diligence investigations, and advising clients
on regulatory compliance matters.

2. COMPENSATION
(a) Base Salary: Employee shall receive an annual base salary of $185,000, payable
in bi-weekly installments, subject to applicable withholdings and deductions.
(b) Bonus: Employee shall be eligible for an annual discretionary bonus based on
individual performance and firm profitability, with a target bonus of 20% of base salary.
(c) Benefits: Employee shall be entitled to participate in all benefit plans offered by
Employer, including health insurance, dental coverage, 401(k) retirement plan, and
paid time off as described in the Employee Handbook.

3. AT-WILL EMPLOYMENT
Employment under this Agreement is at-will. Either party may terminate the employment
relationship at any time, with or without cause, and with or without notice, subject to
the severance provisions of Section 5.

4. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION
(a) Non-Compete: For a period of twelve (12) months following termination, Employee
shall not engage in the practice of corporate law within a 50-mile radius of any office
of the Employer.
(b) Non-Solicitation: For a period of eighteen (18) months following termination,
Employee shall not solicit any client of the Employer or recruit any employee of the
Employer.

5. SEVERANCE
Upon termination without cause, Employee shall receive:
(a) Continuation of base salary for six (6) months;
(b) COBRA health insurance premium payments for six (6) months;
(c) Pro-rated bonus for the year of termination.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work product, inventions, and intellectual property created by Employee during
the course of employment shall be the sole property of the Employer. Employee hereby
assigns all rights, title, and interest in such intellectual property to Employer.

7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration
in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The arbitration
shall take place in the State of Delaware.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
